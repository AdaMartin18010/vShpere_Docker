**AI调度数学模型**:

\[
\text{Optimal Placement} = \underset{node \in Nodes}{\arg\max} \left( \alpha \cdot P(node) + \beta \cdot C(node) - \gamma \cdot L(node) \right)

- \( \alpha, \beta, \gamma \): AI学习的权重
